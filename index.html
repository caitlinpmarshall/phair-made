about us

Phair Made launched in the fall of 2020 when Kate whipped up another pair of mittens for her kids. The colorful, cozy creations caught the eye of friends and classmates, and soon Kate was sewing mountains of mittens! We couldn't keep this winter wear treat to ourselves, so Phair Made was created to bring warmth to chilly paws everywhere.

And we're growing! Kate is bringing her years of experience in detail-oriented and well-crafted hand sewing to the warmer months, too. Waterproof rainpants in bright prints are the perfect partner in childhood adventures. Let's go tromping through puddles, mud, woods, tidepools and outdoor classrooms. You name it, these pants can take it!
